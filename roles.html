Roles

Among Us is played with many different roles. Here are a few and what they do:

Vanilla (Regular) Among Us:

Role Name Team Description Abilities
Crewmate Crewmates Ordinary, Normal N/A
Engineer Crewmates The crew's fixer-upper Can use the map's vent system
Scientist Crewmates The "Medical Professional" Can access Vitals from anywhere
Impostor Impostors Standard "Bad Guy" Can use the vent system, sabotage and kill Crewmates
Shapeshifter Impostors Decieve everyone Everything a normal Impostor can do plus morphing into any crewmate at will
Ghost Ghosts (Crewmate/Impostor) A dead Crewmate/Impostor. You become a ghost after being voted out or killed off Float around, spectate, watch everyone else play
Guardian Angel Ghosts (Crewmate) Sometimes, a dead crewmate becomes an angel. Save others! Cast a shield around any player, guarding them from possible harm

There are many mods (modifications) which allow players to add in more roles. One of the most popular is the "Town Of Us" mod, which adds in a whole bunch of roles for peopel to play as.

Modded Among Us (Town Of Us):

Role Name Team Description Abilities
Crewmate Crewmates Ordinary, Normal N/A
Mayor Crewmates The most important(for sure). Can vote multiple times, up to however many votes they've saved
Lovers Crewmates Two players who are linked together. If one dies, the other dies too Get a private chat between them (to communicate)
Sherrif Crewmates Shoot the Impostors on your own. Killing anyone but the impostor causes you to die as well. Kill button
Engineer Crewmates Just lik ethe normal Among Us Engineer Can use the vent system, can also instantly fix sabotages (Modded only)
Swapper Crewmates Switch Can swap votes between two players during a meeting. Player 1's votes will go to player 2, etc.
Investigator Crewmates The "Detective" Can see footprints of other players for a limited amount of time
Time Lord Crewmates Time traveller, basically Can rewind time for all players. Anything done during this time will reverse, including kills.
Medic Crewmates The Doctor. Heal and protect players Can shield any player, making them immortal as long as the Medic is alive. Reporting a body (as the Medic) will give clues towards the killer
Seer Crewmates Become psychic See whether people are good, evil or neutral. Depedning on settings, the Seer may also see a player's actual role.
Spy Crewmates Get more information See everyone's colour on Admin Table, see how long bodies have been dead on vitals
Snitch Crewmates Finish your tasks ASAP Once all their tasks have been completed, the Snitch gets arrows pointing directly to the Impostors. Impostors, however, get an arrow pointing to the Snitch when they(snitch) have one task remaining
Altruist Crewmates Bring back the dead Can revive dead players, sacrificing their own life in the process
Jester Neutral Get voted out. N/A
Shifter Neutral Basically roleless - No tasks, etc. Can swap roles with another player.
Glitch Neutral Be the last one standing - kill everyone else Hack players to stop them from doing tasks, shapeshift into another player
Executioner Neutral Vote out a specific player to win N/A
Arsonist Neutral Douse all the other players and light them on fire - win by igniting everyone N/A
Phantom Neutral Complete all tasks without being caught Half-invisible on death
Janitor Impostors Clean up dead bodies. Sweep up bodies, erasing them from the map
Morphling Impostors Like the "Shapeshifter" role Sample a player to then transform into later on
Camouflager Impostors Erase all identities Turn everyone colourless (grey) and nameless for a short period of time
Miner Impostors Dig up new passages Place down/create new vents
Swooper Impostors Become invisible Turn temporarily invisible, cannot vent
Assassin Impostors Kill during meetings by guessing the role of other players. A wrong guess results in the Assassin's death N/A
Undertaker Impostors Relocate the dead Move around dead bodies, drag and drop them to different parts of the map
Underdog Impostors Stronger on their own Has a long kill cooldown which is shortened when they're the only Impostor remaining
